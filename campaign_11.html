Medium
Strategy
The rhetorical question mechanic — ask something so obvious it becomes impossible to ignore. Modelled on Dollar Shave Club's "Do you think your razor needs a vibrating handle?" The question does the work. The copy explains why the answer is uncomfortable. Skinome's 90-day expiry becomes the proof point, not the flaw.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Rhetorical question the industry cannot answer cleanly
  • Reframes expiry date as evidence of integrity, not poor quality
  • No brand named — the consumer fills in the blank from their own shelf
  • Calm, unhysterical tone makes the point land harder
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Your yogurt expires in 21 days. Your lotion lasts 36 months. Ask yourself why. Skinome: preservative-free, fresh from our Stockholm lab. Best before 90 days.

SV: Din yoghurt håller i 21 dagar. Din ansiktskräm håller i 36 månader. Fråga dig varför. Skinome: utan konserveringsmedel, färsk från vårt lab i Stockholm. Bäst före 90 dagar.
