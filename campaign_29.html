Open letter
Stockholm, 2019
Dear Skincare Industry,

After 20 years of studying the human skin, I have reached a conclusion you will not like.

The preservatives you use do not discriminate. They kill the bacteria harming skin, and the bacteria protecting it. Every application is a disruption.

You knew this. You chose shelf life anyway.

I chose differently. No preservatives. A cold chain from lab to consumer. Skincare that expires — because fresh things do.

I am not sorry for what I built. I am only sorry it took this long.

Dr. Johanna Gillbro
Founder, Skinome · Dermatology Researcher · 20 years

Strategy
The open letter is the highest-authority confrontational format. BrewDog did it badly (performative). Patagonia did it brilliantly (credible). Dr. Gillbro's 20-year research record makes this letter unchallengeable — the science behind every sentence is real. Written in her voice, in the first person, it's a manifesto, a resignation, and a founding document simultaneously. This is Skinome's brand story in its most confrontational form.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • First-person founder voice = maximum authenticity and accountability
  • "You knew this. You chose shelf life anyway." — precise, unhedged accusation
  • Italic serif on lined paper makes the letter feel handwritten and personal
  • "I am not sorry" — defiance that's earned by the 20-year record
  • High predicted share rate: emotional, specific, human, controversial
