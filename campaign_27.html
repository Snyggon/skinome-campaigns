Medium
Strategy
The logistics document as luxury statement. A brand that needs a cold chain shipping label is making a claim that most skincare brands literally cannot make. The monospace font (Roboto Mono) signals precision and technical commitment. The final line — "Because we made something worth protecting" — reframes the operational constraint as a value statement.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Format authenticity: this looks like a real logistics label
  • "Reject shipment if conditions unmet" — non-negotiable commitment
  • "Not required. Not present." — zero hedging on preservatives
  • Footer line explicitly names why other brands don't need this
  • "Something worth protecting" — positions Skinome as premium by effort
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Our shipping labels look like this. Cold chain required. No exceptions. Other brands ship at room temperature because they use preservatives. We chose the cold chain instead.

SV: Våra fraktlappar ser ut så här. Kylkedja krävs. Inga undantag. Andra märken kan leverera i rumstemperatur för att de använder konserveringsmedel. Vi valde kylkedjan istället.
