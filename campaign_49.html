Periodic Table
Medium
Strategy
Periodic table format borrowed from chemistry gives the campaign instant scientific credibility. The top four "elements" (red-coded) are conventional skincare's worst offenders; the bottom four (green-coded) are Skinome's defining attributes. Color-coding makes the message legible in under two seconds — no reading required. The closing line does all the editorial work.
Mechanisms
  • Periodic table = scientific authority, familiar from school — high-trust format
  • Element symbols (Pe, Pb, Sf) make preservatives feel clinical and formal, not vague
  • 4+4 grid = clean visual balance; neither side overwhelms the other
  • Legend clarifies without text-heavy explanation — shows, doesn't tell
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Two rows. Eight elements. One decision. Your current moisturizer contains some combination of the top four. Skinome contains only the bottom four — because your skin was built around them. Check your INCI list tonight.

SV: Två rader. Åtta element. Ett beslut. Din nuvarande kräm innehåller en kombination av de fyra översta. Skinome innehåller bara de fyra nedre — eftersom din hud är byggd kring dem. Kolla din INCI-lista ikväll.
