Ingredient Autopsy
Medium
Strategy
Side-by-side autopsy format — clinical, structured, unsparing. The left column (beige/muted) represents the old, the right column (dark) represents Skinome — which inverts the usual "dark = bad" visual convention and forces readers to engage. Five data points across both columns build an airtight case. The verdict at the bottom needs no explanation; it's earned by the time readers reach it.
Mechanisms
  • Clinical "autopsy" framing signals objectivity; readers trust lab reports
  • Dark = Skinome subverts expectation and makes the brand feel authoritative
  • Color-coded findings: muted red for harm, pale yellow for clean
  • "Only one of these is skincare" — identical to Campaign 36, compounds the message
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: We opened two moisturizers and looked at what was actually inside. One was made 18 months ago, preserved with phenoxyethanol. One was made last week. No preservatives. Expires in 90 days. The autopsy doesn't lie.
