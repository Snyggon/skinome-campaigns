Wanted: Phenoxyethanol
Medium
Strategy
Western-style "WANTED" poster applied to a specific preservative — phenoxyethanol — making an abstract ingredient feel like a fugitive. The "charges," "method," and "status" fields replace dry science with narrative. The punchline at the bottom ("Found in Skinome: Not present. Not now. Not ever.") delivers the brand position without a single marketing claim.
Mechanisms
  • Wanted poster = Western mythology; visual shorthand for "this is a criminal"
  • A.K.A. section mocks the industry's euphemisms for the same compound
  • "Still yours" — the most confrontational line in the whole creative
  • Reward section subverts the format: the reward for finding it in Skinome is zero
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Phenoxyethanol is in your cleanser, your moisturizer, probably your eye cream. It kills bacteria — including the bacteria your skin needs. Skinome contains none of it. Zero.
