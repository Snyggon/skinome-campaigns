Strategy
Interactive provocation. "Drop your number in the comments" is a high-engagement CTA that transforms a static ad into a participation game — and every comment tells the algorithm this post is alive. The dark background signals a shift in tone: more assertive, less cozy, but not yet hostile. The blurred ingredient wall creates visual tension and subconscious unease without spelling anything out. This is a comment count machine, and comment count is one of the most powerful organic distribution signals on Instagram.
