Concept
Number-forward typographic impact on pale yellow. The full written-out integer "10,000,000,000" is designed to overwhelm — to make the scale of 10 billion feel real and visceral. Pale yellow background is warm, optimistic, and unmistakably Skinome. Dot pattern adds depth without distraction.

  • BioGaia name-drop under the number adds scientific weight
  • "Right now" creates sense of immediate active benefit
