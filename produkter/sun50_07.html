Concept
Ingredient transparency as creative. The ad IS an ingredient list — dense, scrolling, familiar to any skincare reader. The list fades out at the bottom to reveal a single dark badge: ZERO PRESERVATIVES. The contrast between overwhelming complexity (their formula) and clean simplicity (ours) is the entire message. Appeals strongly to label-readers and ingredient-conscious consumers.
Key Angles
  • Show, don't tell — the ingredient list IS the proof
  • Gradient fade to reveal is a visual payoff — reward for reading
  • Targets the most engaged, high-intent skincare consumers
  • "We checked so you don't have to" — empathetic, trust-building
