Concept
Empathy angle — warm sand tones (#e8d5b7), conversational long-copy. Meets the reader where they are: they have a sunscreen they like. This isn't about shaming them. It's about showing them the one flaw in their otherwise-good routine, and offering the fix. The ingredient chips are a visual device that makes the specific preservatives concrete and recognizable. Closing with "We fixed that" — confident and warm.
Key Angles
  • Opens with empathy, not attack — builds trust before making the case
  • Ingredient chip UI is instantly scannable and technically credible
  • Sand/warm palette feels approachable vs. the confrontational ads
  • "We fixed that" — two words, maximum confidence
