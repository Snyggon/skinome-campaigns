Concept
Layered cross-section diagram of skin. CSS horizontal bands from darkest (surface) to lightest (hypodermis), with white bacteria SVG circles positioned on the surface layer. The visual immediately shows where the bacteria go — and where they don't. Split-panel with copy on the right keeps it clean and digestible.
Key Angles
  • Diagram format educates without condescending
  • Bacteria placement on surface layer is the product's literal selling point
  • Legend "Not the target" subverts the assumption that deeper = better
  • Clean white layout — educational, not advertorial
