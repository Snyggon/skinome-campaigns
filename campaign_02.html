The Food Analogy
Soft
Strategy
The food comparison is the most universally relatable entry point for ingredient literacy. Everyone reads food labels — it's normalized, even expected behavior. This ad gently extends that same logic to skincare without preaching or shaming. The split layout lets the visual do the persuasion work. The Swedish caption grounds it firmly in the local market and signals Skinome as a genuinely Swedish brand speaking to its home audience first.
Key Angles
  • Food label comparison — behavior already normalized, zero friction
  • Split design creates instant visual tension without aggression
  • Swedish copy for the domestic market — authenticity signal
  • High save rate expected — "send to a friend" type content
