Pronounce It
Medium
Strategy
The "pronounce it" challenge is a proven content mechanic — it makes the audience pause, attempt the word out loud or in their head, and feel the cognitive weight of complexity. That mental friction is the message. The closing line "If you can't say it, should it be on your face?" delivers the emotional gut-punch gently — as a question, not an accusation. Still warm enough to be widely shareable, just edgy enough to provoke genuine reflection. Bridge campaign between the educational middle tier and the harder-hitting final three.
Key Angles
  • Viral mechanic — audiences mentally try to say the words, then share their reaction
  • Function labels (preservative, silicone) do the educational heavy lifting
  • Contrast with Skinome's readable list lands hard at the end
  • "Should it be on your face?" — a question that opens debate without closing minds
