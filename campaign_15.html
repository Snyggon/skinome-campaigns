Medium
Strategy
Pure visual contrast. The purity of Skinome's water origin (Dalarna, Sweden — three words) set against the density of a conventional ingredient list. No argument needed. The eye does all the work. Highlighted preservatives in the list draw the attention without requiring the audience to read every word.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Volume contrast: 3 words vs. 26 ingredients
  • Highlighted preservatives create a trail of concern
  • Dalarna origin anchors Skinome in natural purity
  • "Our list is 6 items. Theirs is 26." — The only copy needed
  • No brand named — consumer recognizes their own product
