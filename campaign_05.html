Strategy
This campaign does something counterintuitive and brave: it leads with what looks like a weakness. A 3-month expiry date would sink most brands. Here, it becomes proof of purity. The "Best Before 2028" stamp creates instant cognitive dissonance — that date is far too far away for a product without preservatives. The question "what do you think is in there?" plants the seed without spelling out the answer, which is more powerful than stating it. Medium tension: provocative without being hostile.
Key Angles
  • Reframes a perceived weakness (3-month expiry) as evidence of purity
  • The stamp creates instant visual recognition — familiar format, surprising content
  • Question-based CTA plants doubt without preaching
  • Fridge angle reinforces the fresh/alive positioning unique to Skinome
