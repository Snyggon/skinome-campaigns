Strategy
The final campaign is the most minimal — two numbers, one verdict. This is the Apple "1,000 songs in your pocket" compression: the entire Skinome brand proposition in two characters. The asymmetry (36 dark and invisible, 3 bright and white) visually communicates which one is designed for the consumer before a word is read. Maximum impact, minimum complexity. The closing statement: "One was designed for you. One wasn't."
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Two numbers do the entire argument — copy is explanation, not persuasion
  • Dark left (competitor) vs bright right (Skinome) — instant visual hierarchy
  • The smaller number is the brighter one — subverts visual dominance
  • "One was designed for you. One wasn't." — the definitive closing line
  • Best as a series closer: after 19 campaigns of evidence, this lands as a verdict
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Two numbers. One question. Your skincare lasts 36 months because it was designed to survive a warehouse. Ours lasts 3 months because it was designed for you.
