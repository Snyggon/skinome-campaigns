Strategy
The most confrontational ad in the set. Zero softening, zero qualification. Pitch-black aesthetic, near-invisible supporting elements, text stripped to the absolute minimum. The main statement stands completely alone. "We chose your skin" is the brand's thesis distilled to four words — it works precisely because everything else in this ad functions as an indictment. The founder story in the caption (Dr. Johanna Gillbro, 20 years of research, a personal truth discovered through science) adds gravitas and proof. Use this ad sparingly — it is a brand-defining moment, not a recurring tactic.
Key Angles
  • Brand manifesto in four words: "We chose your skin"
  • Pitch-black aesthetic makes every other design choice look like compromise
  • Founder credibility in caption — 20 years research, personal conviction
  • Deploy sparingly — this is a punctuation mark, not a paragraph
