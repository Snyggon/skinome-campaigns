The Receipt
Tough
Strategy
Dollar Shave Club energy applied to Swedish skincare. The itemized receipt is a format humans viscerally understand — we all read receipts. Turning the skincare price breakdown into a receipt forces the audience to confront the gap between what they assume they're paying for and what they're actually paying for. The dark humor is in the math. No attack on a specific brand. Just the structure of an industry.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Format familiarity makes the numbers land immediately
  • The math is the argument — no editorial needed
  • "Brand tax" is the most shareable line item
  • Closing CTA frames Skinome as the alternative without self-promotion
  • Receipt format borrowed from finance, not beauty — pattern interrupt
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: Your 890 SEK moisturizer: 15 SEK of ingredients that touch your skin. The rest is packaging, margins, marketing. That's how the industry works. We chose differently.
