Strategy
The parallel timeline is the most damning format in confrontational marketing — put the evidence next to the response and let the contrast indict. The Patagonia playbook: let reality speak. "Nothing. Nothing. Nothing. Skinome." — the final row is the brand's entire origin story compressed into one word. Dr. Gillbro's founding year (2019) as the industry's failure to act made visible.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Parallel columns — science vs. industry action
  • Repetition of "Nothing." builds to a damning rhythm
  • One word on the final row: "Skinome." — the exception
  • Readable as a list — each line compounds the accusation
  • Earned credibility: Gillbro's scientific record makes this unchallengeable
