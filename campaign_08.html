Strategy
Deliberately confrontational opening — "Congratulations. You just paid for preservatives." The sarcastic framing creates a genuine jolt, but the body copy pivots immediately to education rather than shaming. This is the crucial distinction: the audience feels informed, not attacked. Dark palette, stark type, gold accent bar signal premium challenger brand energy. This is Skinome taking a real, unapologetic commercial stance. The Swedish caption is essential here — it adds authenticity and moderates the confrontation with the softer Swedish directness. This ad will earn strong engagement from loyalists who already agree.
Key Angles
  • Sarcastic "Congratulations" opener — scroll-stopping but immediately clarified
  • Pivots to education: supply chain economics, not skin benefit
  • 23 vs 6 stat box makes the argument data-driven and credible
  • Swedish caption humanizes the brand: "researchers, not marketers"
