Visual Strategy
Repeating the "90" across the entire background turns the expiry number into a brand signature — the same way luxury houses use monogram patterns. The security-paper watermark aesthetic signals value, authenticity, and precision engineering. Rich dark teal is premium without being cold; it reads as earthy and scientific at once. The center white circle acts as a seal or hallmark — "90 Days. Because fresh." is the brand's simplest, most confident statement, delivered as a stamp.
