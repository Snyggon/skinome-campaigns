Data Mosaic — Colorful Cell Grid
Medium
Visual Strategy
The mosaic grid format makes the ingredient count argument visceral and visual — 6 vibrant green squares versus a dense wall of grey instantly communicates the simplicity advantage without requiring any ingredient literacy. The visual weight disparity (6 vs 27+) creates the same emotional response as "6 vs 27" in text, but more immediately. Color carries the entire argument: green is living, active, intentional; grey is bulk, filler, industrial. The data visualization aesthetic appeals to analytically-minded consumers who distrust marketing but trust data. Excellent shareability — designed to be screenshotted and captioned.
