Medium
Visual Strategy
The deep navy palette signals scientific authority — the same visual language as Nature journals and research institute branding. A white circle acts as both a microscope lens and a petri dish, creating immediate science-world recognition. The pale green microbiome organisms inside the white field add biological specificity without requiring photographic imagery. Concentric rings suggest precision, depth, and the layered nature of research.

Full Caption
Dr. Johanna Gillbro spent 20 years researching skin science. The conclusion: conventional skincare was working against the microbiome it claimed to protect. Skinome was the answer she couldn't find elsewhere.
