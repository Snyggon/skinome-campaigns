Visual Strategy
The clinical report format weaponizes the visual language of safety data sheets and lab results — familiar to anyone who reads ingredient labels. Red and green are maximally unambiguous: danger vs. safety. The striped header echoes hazard tape, creating instinctive urgency without explicit alarm. SVG check and cross icons deliver instant legibility at feed-scroll speed. This format targets ingredient-aware consumers who would recognize phenoxyethanol and methylisothiazolinone by name.
