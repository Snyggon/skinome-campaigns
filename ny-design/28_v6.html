Neon Research — Dark Science Drama
Medium
Visual Strategy
The neon year stack turns research milestones into data-visualization aesthetic — familiar from tech dashboards, genomics research tools, and scientific terminal interfaces. The progressive color shift from dim purple through bright cyan to full green maps research intensity to visual brightness, making the culmination in 2019 feel literally illuminated. The subtle grid background suggests a measurement plane. This variant attracts data-literate, science-minded consumers who distrust beauty industry aesthetics.

Dr. Johanna Gillbro spent 20 years researching skin science. The conclusion: conventional skincare was working against the microbiome it claimed to protect. Skinome was the answer she couldn't find elsewhere.
