Traffic Light — Universal Signal Colors
Medium
Visual Strategy
The traffic light is one of humanity's most universal and instinctively understood visual systems — red means danger, green means safe. By mapping preservative ingredients directly to traffic light positions, the ad bypasses rational skepticism and triggers gut-level recognition. The dark charcoal background makes the colored lights pop with maximum contrast, and the green glow on Skinome's circle signals active, living safety. Targets broad audiences — no ingredient knowledge required to understand the message immediately.
