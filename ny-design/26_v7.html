Visual Strategy
The cold-press juice aesthetic speaks directly to a health-conscious consumer who already applies fresh-ingredient logic to their diet — and is ready to apply it to their skin. Warm citrus-to-gold gradient signals energy, vitality, and freshness without feeling pharmaceutical. The white product label format borrows from premium food packaging; the customer reads it the same way they read the back of a juice bottle: looking for what's in it. No preservatives lands like a badge.
