Visual Strategy
The donut chart is the native graphic form of data journalism — it appears in the Economist, FiveThirtyEight, and every credible science publication. Using it here elevates the research story from anecdote to evidence. The progressive greying of 19 segments makes each year feel like accumulated work; the single vivid green segment makes 2019 feel inevitable rather than arbitrary. The cream background and infographic layout give this the clean authority of a scientific explainer — highly shareable by an informed audience.
