Expiry as Pride
Medium
Visual Strategy
Deep navy creates authority and gravitas — this is a brand that has nothing to hide and everything to stand behind. The bright green "EXPIRE." is deliberately jarring against the dark background: it interrupts the eye and forces a re-read. The stark comparison between Skinome's preservative list (None.) and a competitor's ingredient string creates the gut-punch of the piece. Color here carries pure meaning: green equals living, the faded grey preservative list is dead chemistry.
