Visual Strategy
The farmers market aesthetic reframes skincare as fresh produce — something you'd find next to organic greens, not a pharmaceutical shelf. The produce sticker circle is an instantly legible format that signals "fresh and perishable" without a word of explanation. Scattered organic leaf and drop forms in low-opacity white create depth and reinforce the living, botanical identity. Dark green strip grounds the composition the way a market stall's chalkboard would.
