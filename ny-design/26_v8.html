Medium
Visual Strategy
The Polaroid format is the most human and personal of any photo format — it signals intimacy, memory, and authenticity. Placing "90" as an overexposed photograph inside the frame turns the expiry number into something to be kept and remembered, not feared. The handwritten-style caption in the white strip is the emotional gut punch: it's the phrase you'd write to yourself. Warm sand tones throughout create a domestic, unhurried feeling — this belongs to real life, not a retail shelf.
