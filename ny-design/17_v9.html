Visual Strategy
Swedish blue and yellow are globally recognized national identity colors — borrowing this palette directly associates Skinome with Swedish design values: honesty, precision, quality without pretension. The clean horizontal band structure is pure Scandinavian modernism: structured, uncluttered, trustworthy. The yellow accent line and vertical divider create strong visual rhythm without complexity. This variant has particular resonance for Skinome's Swedish domestic market and its strong Norwegian market, where Scandinavian design credentials carry premium weight.
