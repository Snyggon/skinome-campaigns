Visual Strategy
The domestic warmth of a kitchen-wall background makes this feel personal, lived-in, and real — the opposite of a clinical beauty ad. The CSS fridge illustration with magnets and a sticky note brings the product's cold-chain story into an everyday human space. This is for a person who cares about what goes in their fridge as much as what goes on their skin. The sticky note format (handwritten-feel, slightly askew) reads as authentic and unpolished.
