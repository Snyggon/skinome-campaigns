Star Chart — Constellation / Night Sky
Medium
Visual Strategy
Astronomy and biology share the same visual vocabulary — dots, networks, connections between distant points. Mapping research milestones to a constellation turns a timeline into something cosmic and inevitable. The progression in star size mirrors the accumulation of evidence over time. The gold starburst on 2019 functions as a north star — a fixed destination toward which all previous points were always pointing. This design works especially well with audiences who respond to science-as-wonder rather than science-as-authority.

Dr. Johanna Gillbro spent 20 years researching skin science. The conclusion: conventional skincare was working against the microbiome it claimed to protect. Skinome was the answer she couldn't find elsewhere.
