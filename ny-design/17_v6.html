Gradient Drama — Bold Sunset Split
Medium
Visual Strategy
A full-bleed diagonal gradient from deep plum to burnt orange creates immediate visual drama — this reads as campaign poster, not a social media asset, which makes it stop-scroll-worthy. The overlapping Venn-circle format borrows from academic and data visualization language, giving intellectual credibility to the comparison. The left circle's faded, half-visible text visually enacts the message: 36 months is secondary. The "difference is your skin" overlap creates a moment of discovery for close readers. Targets design-conscious premium beauty consumers.
