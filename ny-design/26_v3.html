Medium
Visual Strategy
Arctic blue creates an immediate sensory association with cold, clean, and clinical — exactly what refrigerated skincare should feel like. The CSS snowflake doubles as both brand symbol and product explanation: this product requires cold because it's alive. The temperature notation "2°C – 8°C" borrows pharmaceutical precision to signal scientific seriousness. Sparse particle elements suggest purity and clean-room environments without decoration for its own sake.
