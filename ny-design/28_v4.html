Research Progression — Color Gradient Arc
Medium
Visual Strategy
The gradient arc is a pure data-visualization metaphor — it shows the research journey as a directional progression, not a static fact. The shift from grey to deep green maps perfectly to the meaning: grey suggests uncertainty and early hypothesis, green signals growth, discovery, and living systems. A clean cream background keeps the graphic legible and credible. The enormous "20" below the arc anchors the visual with a single, undeniable number before the copy provides context.

Dr. Johanna Gillbro spent 20 years researching skin science. The conclusion: conventional skincare was working against the microbiome it claimed to protect. Skinome was the answer she couldn't find elsewhere.
