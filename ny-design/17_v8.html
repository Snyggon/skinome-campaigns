Visual Strategy
Dark mode aesthetics perform strongly with younger, design-literate Instagram audiences who associate the neon glow style with tech, gaming, and high-end brands. The visual contrast is maximally expressive: the conventional "36 months" barely glows, barely exists — it's fading into irrelevance. Skinome's "90 DAYS" blazes with cyan energy, dominating the right half. The CSS grid lines beneath everything read as a data visualization background, giving the message scientific credibility. Strongest with 25-35 male and female demographics who follow skincare science accounts.
