Magazine Cover — Bold Editorial
Medium
Visual Strategy
The magazine cover format borrows the credibility of print media — TIME, Wired, scientific journals — without requiring photography. The stark black header bar signals authority and editorial seriousness while the white body keeps the gradient circle legible. The pull quote functions exactly as it would on a real magazine cover: a single sentence that makes a reader stop, disagree, or want to know more. Highest predicted engagement for a literate, educated skincare audience that trusts institutions.
