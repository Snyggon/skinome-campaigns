Visual Strategy
Swedish national colors carry instant cultural meaning — they signal reliability, transparency, and functional design. The flat three-band structure borrows directly from classic Swedish government communication design and IKEA's visual language, making this feel both authoritative and approachable. The oversized "20" alongside a simplified human face graphic personalizes the statistic — the number belongs to a person, not an institution. No gradients means maximum reproducibility and boldness at small sizes.
