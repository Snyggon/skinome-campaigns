Visual Strategy
Duotone split-field is a staple of high fashion editorial photography — it positions Skinome in the same visual territory as Vogue, not Boots. Burgundy is the color of maturity, confidence, and luxury; champagne is softness and warmth. The hard horizontal line between them is the tension that makes you stop: dark and light, bold number above, quiet wit below. The "No preservatives were harmed" line is the campaign's most quotable moment — delivered in italics to earn its irony.
