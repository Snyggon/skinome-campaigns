Visual Strategy
Industrial safety yellow is one of the most scroll-stopping colors in any feed — it signals urgency and danger before a single word is read. The subversion is the payload: the "warning" is not a danger, it's the proof of quality. Reusing a familiar regulatory format (the hazard label) gives instant credibility and pattern recognition. The diagonal stripe texture adds authentic industrial texture without cluttering the message.
