Botanical Science — Nature Meets Research
Medium
Visual Strategy
Forest green connects the biology of skin with the biology of nature — a single visual metaphor that makes microbiome science feel organic and intuitive. The cream specimen circle reads as both a microscope slide and a botanical illustration plate, bridging two centuries of scientific inquiry. Year markers arranged as a constellation suggest research as a patient process of discovery, not a sudden insight. The gold star on 2019 signals arrival, not just completion.
Caption
The skin microbiome is as complex as an ecosystem. She spent 20 years learning to protect it.
Full Caption
Dr. Johanna Gillbro spent 20 years researching skin science. The conclusion: conventional skincare was working against the microbiome it claimed to protect. Skinome was the answer she couldn't find elsewhere.
