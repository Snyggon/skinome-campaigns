Visual Strategy
Data visualization is inherently authoritative — it asks the viewer to read and reason, not just feel. The color-coded timeline is instantly legible: vivid green equals alive and fresh, washed grey equals dead and chemical. Placing Skinome's expiry at the 25% mark visually demonstrates how brief — and how intentional — fresh skincare's life is. The comparison boxes at the bottom close the argument in a format that's almost impossible to argue with.
