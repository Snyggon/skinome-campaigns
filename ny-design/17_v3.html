Medium
Visual Strategy
The cold-chain aesthetic makes Skinome's refrigeration story visceral — you feel the product's freshness before reading a word. Deep navy with cyan glow creates an environment that reads as clinical, cold, and uncompromising. The CSS fridge shape is an immediate physical metaphor: this product lives in a cold chain, not a warehouse. Crystalline grid overlay suggests both laboratory precision and frozen purity. Targets consumers who already know Skinome ships cold and rewards their trust with visual confirmation.
