Strategy
Newspaper front-page format transforms Skinome's message into investigative journalism. The sepia/aged paper aesthetic reinforces the idea that this has been happening for decades — the scandal is old news that consumers just didn't know. The pull-quote becomes the kicker: a single sentence that reframes 90-day expiry as proof of quality, not a flaw.
Mechanisms
  • Newspaper masthead = instant credibility and shareability (screenshot-worthy)
  • "Breaking" framing triggers urgency around a long-standing truth
  • Two-column layout mimics real editorial journalism; creates authenticity
  • Pull-quote delivers the brand punchline without the brand seeming self-promotional
