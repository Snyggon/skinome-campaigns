Strategy Notes
Strikethrough typography is one of the highest-performing formats for reframe content — the visual act of crossing out a belief mirrors the psychological act of rejecting it. Three parallel rows create rhythm and completeness; readers intuitively feel "all three" before they finish processing the first. The numbered structure adds credibility and encourages screenshots. This format consistently drives saves and shares among audiences who enjoy educational content.
