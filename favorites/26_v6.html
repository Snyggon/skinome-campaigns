Ours Goes Bad. On Purpose.
v6 — Side-by-Side Cartons
Medium
Strategy
Direct comparison without naming competitors. The two-carton format makes the priority contrast visceral: logistics vs. skin, preservatives vs. actives, 36 months vs. 90 days. By labeling the two cartons simply "Theirs" and "Ours," the creative generalizes the argument to the entire category — no one brand is attacked, but every conventional moisturizer is implicated. The darker band on "Ours" commands the eye and signals ownership of the better outcome.
