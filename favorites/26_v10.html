Ours Goes Bad. On Purpose.
v10 — Expiry as Identity
Medium
Strategy
The word "EXPIRES" becomes the canvas. By flooding the background with the word everyone fears in skincare — written at near-watermark opacity — the creative owns the concept completely. It's a visual reclamation: Skinome takes the most loaded word in its category and makes it the wallpaper. The foreground text is calm and proud, creating contrast between what the brand is expected to hide and what it chooses to celebrate. "This is what honesty looks like" lands with authority because the design has already demonstrated it.
