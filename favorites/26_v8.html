Ours Goes Bad. On Purpose.
v8 — Countdown, Just Numbers
Medium
Strategy
The "90" at 140px is the entire argument. Oversized numerals carry a specific authority: countdown clocks, ticker boards, lab timers. By making the number the entire design, this creative communicates urgency without anxiety — the countdown is a feature, not a threat. The subtle horizontal grid references technical measurement and precision, reinforcing the lab/science positioning. Everything below the number is support copy, not decoration.
