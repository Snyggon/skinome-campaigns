Strategy
The certificate format hijacks a powerful social signal — official recognition — and applies it to scientific conclusion rather than achievement. The double border and corner ornaments establish institutional gravitas without kitsch. The language ("This is to certify that...") creates a formal register that makes the final finding ("Conventional skincare was working against the skin") read like a verdict. The wordmark at the base functions as a signature. Clean lines throughout ensure it reads as elevated, not costume-y.
