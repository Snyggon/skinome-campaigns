We Refrigerate
Because We Respect You.
Medium
Strategy
The cold chain is Skinome's most radical differentiator — and most counterintuitive. No other skincare brand has done this. The confrontational angle: framing refrigeration as an act of respect, not an operational quirk. The cold palette (ice blue, frost) makes the concept visceral. The supply chain breakdown turns a logistics decision into a brand statement.
Confrontational Mechanism
  • Refrigeration framed as moral choice, not operational necessity
  • "No exceptions" language signals conviction, not compromise
  • Supply chain transparency as trust-building confrontation
  • "No preservatives needed when you do it right" — implicit industry indictment
  • Temperature detail (2–8°C) adds scientific credibility
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: We built a cold chain because preservatives were never the answer. From our Stockholm lab to your fridge — temperature-monitored every step.

SV: Vi byggde en kylkedja eftersom konserveringsmedel aldrig var svaret. Från vårt Stockholm-lab till ditt kylskåp — temperaturövervakat varje steg.
