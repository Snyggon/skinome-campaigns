R.I.P. Microbiome
Tough
Strategy
A tombstone memorial for your skin microbiome — dark, macabre, and impossible to look away from. The format turns a scientific concept (microbiome disruption) into an emotional gut-punch. The epitaph lists the killers by name. The closing line — "Give it a reason to come back" — reframes Skinome not as a product but as a restoration. Highest-tension creative in Series 3.
Mechanisms
  • Tombstone format makes the scientific abstract viscerally personal
  • "Until you started taking care of it" — the cruelest irony, delivered gently
  • Named ingredients on the epitaph are specific enough to be recognizable
  • Closing CTA reframes as restoration, not product purchase
Caption (English / Swedish)

EN: The skin microbiome regulates hydration, fights pathogens, reduces inflammation. Most skincare destroys it with preservatives designed to kill bacteria — including the bacteria you need. Skinome is built around it.
